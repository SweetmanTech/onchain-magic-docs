# Leaderboard

### What is the Leaderboard?

The Zora Protocol Rewards Leaderboard is an open source tracker of how much creators are earning on Zora. It shows an ordered list of the top earning creators of Zora Protocol Rewards over the past 24 hours. The leaderboard includes aggregated data from all fields on Zora Protocol Rewards including:

1. total earned by creators. click on it to toggle `ETH` / `USD`.
2. individual creator earnings. click on it to see breakdown by chain.
3. total earned by zora. click on it to toggle `ETH` / `USD`.
4. creator. click on it to view the creator's profile on Zora.
5. time filter for data. defaults to 1 day. shows the past 24 hours of onchain data.

### What are Zora Protocol Rewards?

According to Zora

Protocol Rewards is a split of our the Zora mint fee allowing:

- Creators to monetize their work
- Developers to earn from NFT mints and creations they facilitate
